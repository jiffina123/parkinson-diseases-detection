As of 2024, Parkinson's disease affects over 10 million individuals globally. The prevalence of Parkinson's disease has been increasing over the past decades. Projections suggest a continued rise in cases, with estimates indicating that the number of individuals with Parkinson's disease could exceed 12 million by 2040.
