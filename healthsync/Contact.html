Help Section & FAQs

How do I set a medicine reminder?​
  • Go to the "Add Medicine" section, enter the medicine details, and save. You’ll start receiving reminders based on your input.
What happens if I miss a dose?

Missing a dose of medicine can have different effects depending on the type of medication.Some medications have specific guidelines on what to do if you miss a dose. Check the prescription label or the leaflet that came with your medicine.

How do I edit or delete a medicine reminder?​

Navigate to the "Upcoming Reminders" section, click on the specific reminder, and choose "Edit" or "Delete."
